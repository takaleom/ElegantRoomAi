Welcome to ElegantRoom.AI

ElegantRoom.AI is an innovative artificial intelligence that helps you design your dream room. Simply upload a photo of your room, and our AI technology will provide you with personalized suggestions to transform your space into a beautiful and functional area.

How it Works

Our AI uses advanced computer vision and machine learning algorithms to analyze your room image and identify opportunities for improvement. It then generates a 3D model of your room, taking into account the dimensions, lighting, and layout. Based on this analysis, our AI provides you with a range of suggestions, including furniture placement, color schemes, and decor ideas.

Get Started

To get started, simply upload a photo of your room using the upload button. Our AI will then work its magic to provide you with a personalized room design. You can then explore different design options, save your favorite ideas, and even purchase the suggested furniture and decor items online.
